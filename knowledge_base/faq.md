# Company FAQ

## What is our return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original packaging and unused condition. To initiate a return, contact support@example.com with your order number.

## What are your business hours?
We are open Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST. Our support team is available 24/7 via chat.

## How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also check order status in your account dashboard under "My Orders".

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, UPI, Net Banking, and PayPal. All transactions are secured with 256-bit encryption.

## How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires in 24 hours.
